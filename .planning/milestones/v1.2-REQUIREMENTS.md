## Success Criteria

1. **LCP < 2.5s** on mobile for 75th percentile of users
2. **INP < 200ms** on mobile for 75th percentile of users
3. **CLS < 0.1** for 75th percentile of users
4. **TTFB < 800ms** on mobile for 75th percentile of users
5. **WCAG 2.1 AA** compliance audit passes
6. **Lighthouse score** ≥ 90 on all pages
